### Sensitivity Analysis

We are confident in our projections. The retail analytics market is large ($8.2B by 2027, Gartner) and we are well-positioned with our AI-driven insights platform.

**Downside scenario:** If growth slows to 20% CAGR, Y3 revenue would be approximately $8.9M and the company would still have 24+ months of runway.

That said, we don't see a realistic scenario where growth drops below 25% given our current pipeline and customer expansion metrics.
