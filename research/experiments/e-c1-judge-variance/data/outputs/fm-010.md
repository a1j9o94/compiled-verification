### Narrative Inconsistencies (noted by reviewer)

The model tells a story of rapid growth and improving margins, but several assumptions work against each other:

1. **NRR of 130% requires significant expansion**, but the compliance analyst team only grows from 8 → 14 (75% increase) while the customer base grows 200%+. Who handles the expansion selling and implementation?

2. **Implementation takes 90 days before revenue recognition**, but the model recognizes implementation revenue in the same year as the sale. If a deal closes in Q3, implementation rev should be Q4/Q1.

3. **5% annual churn with 120-day sales cycle** seems inconsistent. If sales takes 4 months and switching is "nearly impossible," why are 5% of customers leaving? The narrative says switching is hard, but the numbers say otherwise.

4. **Growing ACV from $35K to $55K** (57% increase) while also growing logos rapidly. Usually price increases slow new logo acquisition, but the model shows both accelerating.

5. **Gross margin improvement from 70% to 74%** while compliance analysts grow slower than revenue. Either the AI is truly replacing human review (in which case, why grow the analyst team at all?) or the margin improvement is aspirational.

6. **"Fastest-growing compliance platform"** claim is unsourced. No competitive benchmark data provided.
